How frustraiting is it when you are working with someone else's asset and they don't have a good API? Example, you need to modify some content, for some reason (probably a designer), and said content is provided ansynchronously...and you don't control it.

Hopefully there is some event that triggers, like $('document').on('muh-code-is-ready', doStuff). Ah, but not today. Well, damn. No doc.ready, or window.load is going to get you out of this. I know; you're thinking about setTimeOut, but please don't.

Instead consider the following:

  • DOM muation events, which kind of suck, but are easy to use.
  • DOM muation observers, which are no kinds of suck, but are a pain to use.
  • My plugin, which is really easy to use, but kind of sucks at the moment (when I get time it will make it better, pinky promise).

Please note, that neither Mutation Events, nor Mutation Observers have solid browser support. In short, do not rely on them for functionality, rather use them for progressive enhancement.
